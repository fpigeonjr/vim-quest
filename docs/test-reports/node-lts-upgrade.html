Why Upgrade?

Node 20 enters Maintenance LTS in October 2024 and reaches full End of Life in April 2026 — essentially right now. It will receive only critical security patches, no new features or bug fixes.

Node 22 is the current Active LTS release. Node 24 was released in April 2026 but does not become LTS until October 2026 — too early to adopt for a production project.
