Map Regions and Puzzle Beats

Zone Bounds (x,y) Primary Teaching Beat Gating Rule
A. Arrival Clearing x:2-14, y:22-34 Reorient from Zone 1, remind 0/$ anchors are still available. No lock; checkpoint + hint obelisk.
B. Tutorial Lane x:14-34, y:24-32 Short safe loops introducing w forward and b correction. Must clear 3 marker pads to open split branches.
C1. North Canopy x:34-62, y:14-24 Long forward jumps; punish mashing with dead-branch landings. Collect canopy token.
C2. Root Backtrack x:34-62, y:32-42 Intentional overshoot, then recover with b. Collect root token.
D. Echo Arbor Shrine x:62-74, y:22-34 Unlock e; micro test requires landing on word ends. Both branch tokens required.
E. Precision Terraces x:74-90, y:14-42 Mixed lanes combining w b e and occasional 0/$ reset rails. Clear 4 terrace runes to open mini-shrine.
F/G. Lexeme Shrine + Sentence Gate x:90-96, y:22-34 Final sequence validates command fluency under light pressure. Zone completion unlock + route back to hub.

Learning Objectives and Assessment

ID Objective Assessment Signal
LO-2.1 Use w to advance to intended word starts without fallback movement keys. In B and C1, clear 3 consecutive pads with w-only input.
LO-2.2 Use b to recover from overshoot and re-align to target paths. In C2, recover from forced overshoot in <=2 commands, 3 times.
LO-2.3 Use e to land on exact word ends for narrow platforms. D unlock test requires 4 correct endpoint landings with e.
LO-2.4 Choose between w, b, and e based on target position. E terraces complete with <=6 invalid commands across all runes.
LO-2.5 Reinforce prior anchors 0/$ as fast reposition tools. At least 2 mandatory terrace rails require 0 or $ use.
LO-2.6 Demonstrate fluency in a full route under mild pressure. Sentence Gate clear in one attempt window with checkpoint rollback support.
